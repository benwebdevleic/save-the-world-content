--- layout: default title: Reusable period pads ---

One of the things a woman will go through a lot in her life is menstrual pads. They can get very expensive, and some women find them uncomfortable to wear. Fabric menstrual pads are not only more economical and environmentally-friendly, but they are also more comfortable to wear. Made from breathable cotton, they cause less sweating and odor than regular pads. They may also lower the risk of toxic shock syndrome. Best of all, they are easy to make!
